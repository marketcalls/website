üß† The Wizard Technique

Van Tharp's famous technique for instantly changing your emotional state during trading.

When You're in a Bad State (Angry, Fearful, Frustrated):

1

Stand Up & Move

Immediately change your physical position. Movement breaks the emotional pattern.

2

Dissociate - Watch Yourself

Step back mentally and observe yourself as if watching a movie. See yourself sitting there in that emotional state.

3

Model a Super Trader

Imagine how Warren Buffett or your trading hero would handle this exact situation. How would they sit? Breathe? React?

4

Assume Their State

Physically adopt their posture, breathing, and mindset. Become that calm, confident trader.

  • ‚Ä¢ Psychology: Dissociation reduces emotional intensity by 80%
